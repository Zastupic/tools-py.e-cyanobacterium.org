Welcome to DoAB cyano tools!


This site offers tools for analyzing data acquaried by methods routinely used in laboratories working with cyanobacteria, algae, plants or bacteria. The site is developed at Department of Adaptive Biotechnologies (DoAB) of Global Change Research Institute of the Czech Academy of Sciences (CzechGlobe).

The tools enable data processing for the following methods:
  • Fluorescence microscopy
    • Cell counting - round cells
    • Cell counting - filamentous strains
  • PAM Fluorometry
    • Analysis of fast fluorescence transients (OJIP)
  • Fluorescence spectroscopy
    • Analysis of 3D excitation-emission fluorescence maps
  • Confocal microscopy
    • Pigment profiles - round cells
    • Pigment profiles - filamentous strains
    • Cell size - round cells
    • Cell size - filamentous strains
