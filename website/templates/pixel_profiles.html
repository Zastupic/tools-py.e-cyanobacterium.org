Pixel profiles


Welcome to the app for getting pixel profiles of cells on your image!
To analyze pixel profiles of cells on the fluorescence microscopy image, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Select your image
  2. Select cells on your image for the pixel profile analysis
      3. Select expected cell size
      Select the cells
  3. Confirm selection and analyze intensity of pixels across the cellular profiles (with fixed angle of 15°)
