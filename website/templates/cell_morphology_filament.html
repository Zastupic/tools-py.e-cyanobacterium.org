Cell morphology — filamentous strains

At a glance: Upload a fluorescence or brightfield image of filamentous algae or cyanobacteria. The tool automatically segments individual cells and measures aspect ratio, circularity, and eccentricity per cell and per filament. Results are colour-coded on the image and exported to XLSX.

Suitable for fluorescence and brightfield microscopy images of filamentous cyanobacteria and algae. Cells are automatically segmented using watershed-based algorithms and shape descriptors — aspect ratio, circularity, and eccentricity — are computed per cell and per filament.

  1. Upload your image — live preview appears automatically
  2. Enter pixel size (nm)
  3. Tune pre-processing and segmentation parameters; separate filaments must be enabled for per-cell shape measurements
  4. Click Run morphology analysis — the server segments cells and computes shape metrics
  5. Review the colour-coded image (green = rod-like, red = sphere-like) and switch Colour by to inspect circularity or eccentricity
  6. Manually correct missed or spurious cells, then download XLSX
